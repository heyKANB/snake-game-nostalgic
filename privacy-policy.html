Privacy Policy for Snake Game - Nostalgic

Effective Date: January 29, 2025

Last Updated: January 29, 2025

Quick Summary: This Snake game respects your privacy. We only collect minimal data necessary for ads and game functionality. No personal information is stored or shared.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Statistics: High scores, game preferences, and theme selections (stored locally on your device)
  • Advertising Data: Anonymous identifiers for ad personalization through Google AdSense
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, app version for crash reporting and performance optimization

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Microphone or camera access

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Experience: Save your high scores and preferences locally
  • Advertisements: Display relevant ads through Google AdSense
  • App Improvement: Analyze crash reports and performance metrics

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Game data is stored locally on your device
  • No Account Required: No user accounts or cloud storage
  • Security: Standard encryption for any data transmission

4. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data according to their privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense Privacy: https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/48182

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game operates without requiring personal data input.

6. Your Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Sound/Theme Preferences: Adjustable within the game settings

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date.

8. International Users
